Herbal Tea

How to Make Herbal tea
Herbal tea is a delightful and soothing beverage made by steeping herbs, flowers, or other plant materials in hot water. Here's a basic recipe for making herbal tea:

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beverage drinks food

instructions

step 1 : CHOOSE YOUR HERBS

Select the herbs or herbal blend you'd like to use for your tea. You can use a single herb or create a custom blend of herbs to suit your taste. Common choices include chamomile, peppermint, lavender, ginger, hibiscus, and many more.

step 2: COOK

Place herbs in a cup or tea infuser. Pour boiling water over herbs. Steep for 5-7 minutes. Remove herbs or infuser. Sweeten (optional).

step 3: SERVE

Serve your herbal tea immediately.Enjoy it with your favourite meal.

ingredients

1-2 teaspoons of dried herbs (herbs of your choice)

1 cup of boiling water

tools

Teapot or Mug

Dried Herbs

Tea Infuser (Optional)

Boiling Water

Lid or Saucer (Optional)
