Cobb salad

How to Make cobb salad
A Cobb Salad is a classic American salad that's hearty and packed with flavor. It typically includes a variety of ingredients, and you can adjust it to your taste. Here's a basic recipe for a Cobb Salad:

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : breakfast taco food

instructions

step 1 : PREPARE THE DRESSING

In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and pepper. Adjust the seasonings to taste.

step 2: ASSEMBLE THE SALAD

On a large serving platter or individual plates, arrange the chopped romaine and iceberg lettuce as the base. Arrange the hard-boiled eggs, diced tomatoes, diced chicken breast, crumbled cheese, crumbled bacon, diced avocado, and diced red onion in rows or sections on top of the lettuce.

step 3: GARNISH AND SERVE

Drizzle the dressing over the Cobb Salad. If desired, garnish with chopped fresh parsley or chives. Serve the Cobb Salad immediately. You can also serve the dressing on the side for guests to add as they like.

ingredients

2 cups chopped romaine lettuce

2 cups chopped iceberg lettuce

2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped

2 medium tomatoes, diced

1 cup cooked and diced chicken breast

1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese or feta cheese

4 slices of cooked bacon, crumbled

1 avocado, diced

tools

Cutting Board and Knife

Large Salad Platter or Individual Plates

Mixing Bowl

Whisk

Measuring Cups and Spoons

Small Bowl (for dressing)
