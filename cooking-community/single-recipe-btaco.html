Breakfast Taco

How to Make Breakfast taco
"Start your day with a delicious and satisfying breakfast by making these mouthwatering breakfast tacos. In just a few simple steps, you'll have a hearty and flavorful morning meal that's perfect for fueling your day."

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : breakfast taco food

instructions

step 1 : PREPARE THE FILING

Cook the breakfast sausage or bacon in a skillet over medium heat until it's browned and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels. Crumble it once cooled. In the same skillet, you can sauté the diced onions and bell peppers until they're tender and slightly caramelized.

step 2: SCARAMBLE THE EGGS

In a bowl, whisk the eggs until the yolks and whites are well combined. Season with salt and pepper. Heat a separate non-stick skillet over medium-low heat. Add the butter or oil and let it melt. Pour the whisked eggs into the skillet and cook them, stirring constantly, until they're softly set but still slightly creamy. This should take just a few minutes. Remove from heat.

step 3: WARM THE TORTILLAS

In a dry skillet or on a griddle, warm the tortillas over medium heat for about 10-15 seconds on each side. You can also wrap them in a damp paper towel and microwave them for a few seconds until warm and pliable.

step 4: ASSEMBLE THE TACOS

Lay out the warm tortillas on a clean surface. Place a portion of the scrambled eggs on each tortilla. Add a portion of the crumbled sausage or bacon. Sprinkle diced tomatoes, sautéed onions, and bell peppers on top. Top with shredded cheese. Optionally, add a dollop of salsa or hot sauce for some extra flavor.

step 5: SERVE

If desired, garnish the breakfast tacos with chopped fresh cilantro or parsley. Fold the tortillas in half, and your breakfast tacos are ready to be served.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix or All purpose flour

3 bananas

1/2 cup sugar
