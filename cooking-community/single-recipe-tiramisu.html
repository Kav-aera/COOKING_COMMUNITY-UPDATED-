How to Make Tiramisu

30 min.

6 servings

Tags : sweet dessert food

instructions

step 1 : PREPARE THE MASCARPONE FILLING

In a mixing bowl, whisk the egg yolks and sugar until the mixture becomes pale and slightly thick. Add the mascarpone cheese and vanilla extract, then mix until smooth

step 2: WHIP THE CREAM AND COMBINE

In another mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until well combined. Be careful not to overmix; you want a light and fluffy consistency.

step 3: BREW COFFEE AND ASSEMBLE TIRAMISU

Brew a strong cup of coffee and let it cool to room temperature. If desired, you can add coffee liqueur to the coffee for extra flavor. Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture, making sure not to soak them for too long to prevent sogginess. Arrange a layer of dipped ladyfingers in the bottom of your serving dish.

step 4: DEGLAZE THE WINE AND COOK RISOTTO

If using wine, pour it into the pan and stir until it is mostly absorbed by the rice. Begin adding the vegetable broth, one ladleful at a time. Stir continuously and allow the liquid to be absorbed before adding more. Continue this process until the rice is cooked, which should take about 18-20 minutes. The risotto should be creamy with a slight bite to the rice (al dente).

step 5: ADD LAYERS AND FREEZE

Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Add another layer of dipped ladyfingers and top with the remaining mascarpone mixture. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, but overnight is ideal, as it allows the flavors to meld.

step 6: GARNISH AND SERVE

Just before serving, dust the top of the Tiramisu with unsweetened cocoa powder. You can also add chocolate shavings or grated chocolate for decoration, if desired. Use a spatula to serve individual portions, making sure to get both the ladyfingers and the creamy layers.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups Arborio rice

1 small onion, finely chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced

4 cups vegetable broth (approximately)

1/2 cup dry white wine (optional)

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

2 tablespoons olive oil

2 tablespoons unsalted butter

tools

Large Skillet or Wide Saucepan

Grater

Ladle

Vegetable Broth
